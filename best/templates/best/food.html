Ingredients : 1½cup flour, sugar, ⅓cup milk, a little salt, 1 teaspoon yeast sugar, 1 cup water, pizza sauce, tomato ketchup, onion, garlic, salt pepper, onion, bell pepper, button mushrooms, bacon, cheese for pizza, 2 gamgyuls

How

  1. Put a teaspoon of sugar and yeast in warm water at 40°C, put it in an oven set as a fermentation function, ferment it for three to four minutes, and when it swells, put it in flour, add milk, sugar, and salt, and knead it.
  2. Leave it in a warm place for 30 to 40 minutes, and when it rises, remove air by touching it, and spread the dough thinly in an oiled pizza pan.
  3. Make the sauce. Chop onions and garlic and then stir-fry with a little bit of oil, add tomato ketchup, boil slowly, season with salt and pepper, pour it into the prepared dough and spread it out.
  4. Remove the oil after stir-frying bacon and cut it into 1cm pieces and then slice mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, and gamgyuls into shapes.
  5. Place vegetables and sliced cheese for pizza on top of a sauce-coated dough and bake in a lidded pan for 20 minutes.
